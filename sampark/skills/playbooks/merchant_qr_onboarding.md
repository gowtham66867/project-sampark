# Merchant QR Onboarding Playbook

Tone: business-owner framing, focused on practical day-to-day impact on
takings and settlement, not generic "go digital" messaging.

- Lead with what changes for the merchant's daily cash flow: faster
  collection at the counter, and when settlement into their account
  actually happens.
- Be specific and honest about settlement timing and any fees -- do not
  round up or gloss over either.
- Confirm business ownership and the settlement account belong to the
  person being onboarded before describing next steps.
- Never claim the QR code is already active or that a specific settlement
  amount/time is guaranteed -- describe the process, not a promise.
