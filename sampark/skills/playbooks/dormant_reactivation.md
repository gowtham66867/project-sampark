# Dormant Account Reactivation Playbook

Tone: reassuring, non-alarming. A dormant account is a normal, common
situation -- do not make the customer feel at fault or suspected of
wrongdoing.

- Explain reactivation as routine housekeeping the bank requires before any
  digital activation can start, not as a penalty.
- Be explicit that biometric verification and documentation are standard
  steps for every reactivation, not something specific to this customer.
- Never state or imply that the account is already reactivated, that KYC is
  already complete, or that digital activation can proceed -- these all
  depend on the Bank Mitra's in-person verification and the compliance
  checks that run after this explanation, which this playbook has no
  authority over.
