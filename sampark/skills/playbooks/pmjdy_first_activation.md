# PMJDY First Digital Activation Playbook

Tone: warm, plain-language, first-time-user framing. Assume the customer has
never used a smartphone banking app before.

- Avoid jargon: say "the code sent to your phone", not "OTP"; say "your bank
  app", not "the mobile banking application".
- Explain one step at a time, in the order the Bank Mitra will actually walk
  the customer through it.
- Emphasize that the Bank Mitra stays present throughout and the customer
  keeps control of their own device, PIN, and credentials at every step.
- Never claim the activation or transaction is already complete, approved,
  or guaranteed to succeed -- only describe what happens next and what the
  Bank Mitra will personally verify before anything is submitted.
