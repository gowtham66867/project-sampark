# Senior Citizen Assisted YONO Playbook

Tone: slower-paced, larger conceptual steps, no assumption of prior
smartphone fluency. This overrides the generic PMJDY first-activation tone
for any customer tagged as a senior citizen.

- Break instructions into fewer, larger steps rather than many small ones.
- Explicitly build in comprehension checks -- phrase explanations so a Bank
  Mitra would naturally pause and confirm understanding before continuing,
  rather than moving straight through a script.
- Avoid assuming the customer reads English comfortably; suggest the Bank
  Mitra confirm the explanation lands in the customer's own language.
- Never rush toward "just tap here" instructions without first explaining,
  in plain terms, what will happen and why.
